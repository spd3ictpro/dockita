Risk Stratification

error
VERY HIGH RISK

Established ASCVD, DM with target organ damage, or CKD (GFR <30).

warning
HIGH RISK

Markedly elevated single risk factors (TC >8, LDL >4.9) or DM without TOD.

info
MODERATE RISK

Young patients with DM or 10-year risk score 1% to <5%.

check_circle
LOW RISK

Calculated 10-year risk score <1%.

Statin Therapy Intensity

HIGH INTENSITY

Lowers LDL-C by ≥50%

  • Atorvastatin 40 - 80 mg
  • Rosuvastatin 20 - 40 mg

MODERATE INTENSITY

Lowers LDL-C by 30% to <50%

  • Atorvastatin 10 - 20 mg
  • Rosuvastatin 5 - 10 mg
  • Simvastatin 20 - 40 mg

Monitoring Schedule

Baseline

Lipid panel, ALT, and HbA1c before starting statin therapy.

4 - 12 Weeks

Repeat lipid panel to assess adherence and response to therapy.

Every 3 - 12 Months

Periodic assessment of lipids based on clinical judgment and stability.

info

Monitor ALT only if symptoms of hepatotoxicity occur.
