# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Medical officers on duty and general practitioners using dockita during clinical work for quick reference, calculations, and patient education.

## Product Purpose

A daily dashboard providing instant access to Malaysia CPG screening guidelines, clinical calculators, risk scores, geriatric assessment tools, and patient education materials — all in one place without leaving the clinic workflow.

## Brand Personality

Clinical, clean, calm. No-nonsense, professional, and trustworthy.

## Anti-references

- Generic hospital system aesthetics (outdated, cluttered, beige-and-blue EMR interfaces)
- Consumer health app aesthetics (too casual, colorful, gamified)

## Design Principles

1. **Clinical at a glance** — Information must be scannable in seconds. Every screen starts with the tool the doctor needs, not decoration.
2. **Trust through precision** — Layout, spacing, and typography communicate reliability. Clean alignment, restrained color, and consistent patterns build confidence.
3. **Calm under pressure** — No jarring animations, no visual noise. The interface stays quiet and lets the data speak.
4. **Progressive disclosure** — Show the essentials first, reveal details on demand (accordions, tooltips, expandable sections). Don't overwhelm the clinician.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard best practices. Sufficient contrast for clinical environments with varied lighting.
